How to Join

To join the association, you may download a PDF application that can be mailed or faxed. Please find the application form in the following link.

CAEPA Application Form

CAEPA membership is categorized in three types:

1. Life Membership: $200
2. Annual Membership: $30/year
3. Student Membership: $10/year
