  • CAEPA March 4 Talk: Transitioning to Alternative Energy: A Roadmap for 50 States and 139 Countries by Professor Mark Jacobson of Stanford University

    • Global warming, air pollution, and energy insecurity are three of the most significant problems facing the world today. This talk discusses the development of technical and economic plans to convert the energy infrastructure of each of the 50 United States and 139 countries of the world to those powered by 100% wind, water, and sunlight (WWS) for all purposes, namely electricity, transportation, industry, and heating/cooling, after energy efficiency measures have been accounted for.     About the Association

    The Chinese-American Environmental Professionals Association (CAEPA), founded in August 1988 in Oakland, CA, is a non-profit organization made up of professionals, educators, and others who are concerned about improving the environment.
