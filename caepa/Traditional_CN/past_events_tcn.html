2011 SCCAEPA Symposium on Global Emerging Environmental Challenges and Government Responses

Three of our CAEPA members presented at the event. Their abstracts are provided below:

  • Energy Saving through Superior Design and Process Control Abstract - John Chien
  • Challenge and Innovation - Sustainable Remediation of Chlorinated Solvent Contamination Sites n- Deyi Hou
  • Green Remediation and RPO Abstract - Jim Leu
