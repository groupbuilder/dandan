Recent Events

CAEPA February 2013 Seminar
Place: 7700 Edgewater Drive, Suite 260, Oakland, CA
Date: February 16(Saturday), 11:00AM - 3:30PM

2013 CAEPA Environmental Contest for Asian American Students are held on March 29, 2013. Please see announcement flyer for more details.

The visit of Former Vice President of Republic of China in Taiwan - Dr. Hsiu-lien Lu to the San Jose/Santa Clara Water Pollution Control Plant and the Silicon Valley Advanced Water Purification Center on December 8, 2012.

CAEPA members attended the Greenbuild International Conference and Expo in San Francisco from November 14 through 16, 2012 and photographed with Chair of World Green Building Council Mr. Wall.

CAEPA had a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with AsianWeek Foundation (AWF) on October 31, 2012. CAEPA will have the AWF as a community sponsor to cover the scholarship program period of October 31, 2012 to November 1, 2013.

CAEPA members attended the Stanford-Taiwan Biomedical Forum 2012 on October 12, 2012 in Palo Alto.

CAEPA members visited new Director of Science and Technology Division Dr. Ting-An Wang of Taipei Economic and Cultural Office on October 5, 2012 in Santa Clara.

CAEPA members attended the reception of the 63th anniversary of the founding of the People’s Republic of China on September 21, 2012 in San Francisco.

CAEPA sponsored the Southern California Chinese American Environmental Protection Association's (SCCAEPA's) 5th Symposium on Global Emerging Environmental Challenges and Government Responses, held in Los Angeles on August 9 through 11, 2012. CAEPA members served as moderator and speakers.

CAEPA held a site tour to visit SFPUC Sunol Valley Water Treatment Plant on July 28, 2012. Mr. Ravi Krishnaiah and Mr. Mike Price gave a presentation and the plant tour.

The 2012 Cross-Strait Environmental Summit was successfully held in Taipei, Taiwan from April 22 to 24, 2012. It was a great conference attended by about 150 - 200 people from China, Taiwan, Hong Kong, and U.S. CAEPA members served as speakers in various areas including environmental education, ecological conservation, noise control, low carbon policy, and green industry.

The “After Three Gorges Dam” international symposium was successfully completed at University of California (UC) - Berkeley from April 13 to 14, 2012. As a co-sponsor to assist the Department of Landscape Architecture and Environmental Planning at UC Berkeley, CAEPA member served as moderator and translators in various capacities for this 2-day event.
