The Year 2013-2014 Board of Directors Election

There are six newly elected directors this year. They are Sue Chau, Kevin Jim, Ray Yep, Shin-Roei Lee, Diane Wang, and Ivy Tao. Together with the returning BODs, the new directors are believed to infuse new ideas to help achieve the most important event of 2013 – the international conference which will substitute the 2013 annual meeting. The annual meeting will be resumed in 2014. In order to prepare for the international conference, the BOD decided in November 2012 to extend the current officer term from one year to two years.

About the Association

The Chinese-American Environmental Professionals Association (CAEPA), founded in August 1988 in Oakland, CA, is a non-profit organization made up of professionals, educators, and others who are concerned about improving the environment.
