Detailed Conference Program

Session C: Clean Up Our Mess For the Future Generations: Our Air, Soil and Groundwater

Session 1: Opening Session
Session 2: A panel discussion to set the stage for the conference, Rogional board? Others?
Session 3: What is our vision? What will be the drivers for air pollution control, soil And groundwater remediation?
Session 4: Stories of our successes and failures- What did we learn from our effort on air pollution control?
Session 5: Why some soil remediation did not work? What did we learn?
Session 6: Are we successful in cleaning up our groundwater? Need new sustainable technologies? How clean is clean?
Session 7: What is the future of Remediation? - Growing? Cycling? Or Dying?
Session 8: What are we taking home from this conference? Conclusions and Recommendations

  • Investigation & remediation technologies for soil & groundwater
