Conference Program

The conference will have an opening session followed by three parallel trains

Train A: A Sustainable Future - How Do We Transform Our Vision Into Reality

  • Sustainability initiative: What is our sustainable future look like?
  • Going Green: Greener Initiatives, approaches and strategies…
  • Recent sustainability initiatives: federal, state, and local; domestic and global
  • Sustainable infrastructure: latest research and best management practices

Train B: Re-invent Our Wastewater Infrastructure

  • Wastewater management strategy: how do we replace our systems with 21st century technologies?
  • Maximize the reuse and recovery of our precious resources: water, land, energy, solids, and nutrients!
  • Waste no more! Resources recovery from wastewater and biosolids
  • Reuse our most reliable water resource!
  • Wastewater Best Management Practices

Train C: Clean up our mess for the future generations: our air, soil and groundwater

  • What is our vision? What is our remediation strategy?
  • Stories of our successes and failures: What did we learn from our experiences?
  • Are we successful in cleaning up our soil and groundwater? What are the issues?
