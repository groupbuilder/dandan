Lodging

The conference and training will be held at DoubleTree by Hilton at San Francisco International Airport, located at 835 Airport Boulevard, Burlingame, CA 94010. The hotel is close to the airport and the hotel runs a free bus service to and from the airport approximately every 30 minutes. A block of 30 rooms have been reserved for the conference during the conference time (10/9 - 10/13) at the following discounted group rates:

Room (per room per night rate) Single rate Double rate
Non-smoking king $125 $125
Non-smoking two double beds $125 $125

 

The above rates do NOT include tax, fees, tips, etc.
Please call the hotel at 650‐344‐5500 to reserve your room(s). Please indicate CAEPA in your reservation.
